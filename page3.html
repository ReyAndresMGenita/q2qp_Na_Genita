ABOUT US

Founded in 2015, the Philippines Partnership for Sustainable Agriculture (PPSA) stands as the organization that helps the farmers.

PPSA is at the forefront of helping address the pressing issues in agriculture by providing a platform for various stakeholders to engage, discuss, collaborate and bring to life initiatives and best practices of individual companies into a collective action aimed at improving the lives of smallholder farmers.

Mission

We harness the collective strength of agricultural value chain players towards transforming food systems to become climate positive and inclusive through market-driven solutions.

Vission

The Enabler of multi-stakeholder partnerships for sustainable and inclusive food systems that empower and transform smallholder farmers and fishers to be self-reliant and resilient.
